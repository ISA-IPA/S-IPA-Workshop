Job Responsibilities: 
         • Preparation of Bills of Material by identifying the list of externally procured items; in-house production items and the items that are manufactured from Vendors are studied through drawings. 
         • Co-ordinate with materials department for the shortage of materials. 
         • Prepare and arrange daily Shop floor material requirements. 
         • Capacity planning for all in-house manufacturing activities.

         • Be a part of cross-functional team involving various departments for streamlining activities and for better co-ordination and information flow within the factory with proper documents.
